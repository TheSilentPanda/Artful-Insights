Rombutan

BIOGRAPHY

This Filipino artist has gained internet fame from his marvelous artworks that depict the thoughts and struggles of what most college students experience. His works would contain a one liner with a semi-realistic drawing that are either witty or deep. It speaks to us, Millennials, in some way. His works are usually symbolically exaggerated, but is still able to depict the main thought clearly. Rombutan’s works are mostly done with pastel colors that inclines a lot with the style of pop art. It’s like a beautiful masterpiece. He also uses his artworks as a way to express his thoughts about some issues in the Philippines, stating his own opinion. Just by seeing his works, you would know that this artist is skillfully remarkable.
