Gabriel Picolo

BIOGRAPHY

Known for his most famous work, “Icarus and the Sun”, Gabriel has gained such popularity especially through social media, specifically with the young ones, with the content of his artworks. His skill with ink art and digital art is remarkable, and not to mention the subtle messages he portrays as well in most of his artworks. He usually draws about first-world problems, surrealism, semirealism, and also does a lot of fanart, drawn in his own style (and may have also drawn characters he shipped together). He seems to be a huge fan of Harry Potter, Teen Titans (specifically Beast boy with Raven), and Studio Ghibli & Disney films. Millennials have given such appreciation to his artworks, especially since most of his works, not only would be nostalgic but it would also bring us either the ultimate feels or it would drown us with romance. His works is definitely something to look out for. You would not be able to not look twice at his works with your jaw dropping and your heart etiher skipping a beat or breaking.
